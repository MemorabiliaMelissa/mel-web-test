A Film Title About Dreams Folding Away

Client

Year

Emma Smith

2018

Emma Smith is a film graduate who needed a film title sequence at the start of her short film, ‘Starry Night’. Starry Night told a story of a girl who was stuck between childhood and adulthood as well as reality and dreams. For the creation of the film title, the focus was on the theme of guilt and the reality of her situation.
