Helping Parents to Talk to their Children About Cyber Safety

Client

Year

CyberSafe Ireland

2018

CyberSafe Ireland is an Irish children’s charity for online safety. They requested a designed microsite in order to educate parents of the potential dangers of children being online. My team mate, Holly Smyth, and I believed that communication is key and we tried to achieve communication where a child would feel comfortable to talk openly to their parent.
